INSIDE OUT INFORMATION
NAME: Anger
DESCRIPTION: Anger feels very passionately about making sure
things are fair for Riley. He has a fiery spirit and tends to explode
(literally) when things don’t go as planned. He is quick to overreact
and has little patience for life’s imperfections.
NAME: Fear
DESCRIPTION: Fear’s main job is to protect Riley
and keep her safe. He is constantly on the lookout for
potential disasters, and spends time evaluating the possible dangers,
pitfalls and risk involved in Riley’s everyday activities.
There are very few activities and events that Fear does not find to be
dangerous and possibly fatal.
NAME: Disgust
DESCRIPTION: Disgust is highly opinionated, extremely
honest and prevents Riley from getting
poisoned –both physically and socially. She keeps a careful
eye on the people, places and things that
Riley comes into contact with – whether that’s
broccoli or last year’s fashion trend.
NAME: Joy
DESCRIPTION: Joy is the main character of Inside Out.
When Riley was born Joy is the first Emotion to materialize
inside her mind. She appears to be happy all the time and full of energy.
NAME: Sadness
DESCRIPTION: Sadness is a character in Inside Out.
She is the blue Emotion and one of the five
Emotions inside the mind of Riley, along
with Joy, Fear, Anger, and Disgust.
